About AE Renewable Ltd

AE Renewable Ltd is a leading renewable energy and electrical engineering company committed to providing reliable, efficient, and cost-effective energy solutions for homes, businesses, institutions, and industries across Nigeria.

We specialize in the design, supply, installation and maintenance of solar power systems, inverter and battery solutions, electrical installations, CCTV surveillance systems and smart home automation.

Our team of experienced engineers and technicians delivers quality solutions that promote energy independence, operational efficiency and a greener tomorrow.

OUR MISSION

To provide sustainable, reliable, and affordable renewable energy solutions that improve lives, empower businesses, and support national development.

OUR VISION

To become Nigeria's most trusted renewable energy company, delivering innovative energy solutions that drive sustainable growth and energy independence.
