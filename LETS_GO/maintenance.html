System Care Overview

Reliable performance demands continuous oversight and preventive upkeep.

Regular maintenance ensures your renewable energy and electrical infrastructure operates at peak efficiency. Our team of certified engineers carries out rigorous multi-point inspections, system diagnostics, performance tuning, and thermal imaging to prevent downtime and extend equipment life.

Comprehensive Support Offerings

Targeted upkeep programs engineered across all clean-energy and smart tech categories.

Solar Array Optimization

De-dusting, panel string voltage checks, and module thermal scans to maximize total yield and clean power production.

Battery Bank Health Audit

Cell balancing checks, state-of-health (SOH) diagnostics, and charge controller calibration for Lithium and Gel systems.

Inverter & Hybrid Diagnostics

Firmware upgrades, internal dust extraction, load analysis, and thermal testing to prevent inverter faults.

Electrical Safety Check

Inspection of surge protection devices (SPDs), circuit breakers, cable integrity, and earthing ground resistance tests.

CCTV & Security Servicing

Camera lens cleaning, NVR storage formatting, network connection re-syncing, and night-vision IR testing.

Smart Home Automation Calibration

Zigbee/Z-Wave mesh re-indexing, smart hub firmware updates, and automated scenario triggers fine-tuning.
