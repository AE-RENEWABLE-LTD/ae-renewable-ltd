PROJECT DESCRIPTION

An intelligent 8kVA-8000W solar power backup system designed to provide reliable energy for Resident applications. The system is configured with a minimum 15kWh lithium battery bank and 8 units of 750W solar panels, enabling substantial daytime solar generation and extended nighttime backup. The system includes all major installation accessories, protection devices, and monitoring components for safe and efficient operation.
