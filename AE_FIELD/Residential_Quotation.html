PROJECT DESCRIPTION

This proposal provides for the replacement of the existing tubular battery bank and transformer-based IPS inverter with a modern 16kVA DEYE Hybrid Inverter and 60KWH DEYE STACK battery system, while retaining the existing solar PV array, subject to compatibility and site assessment. The upgraded system delivers intelligent energy management, higher efficiency, longer battery life, and reliable uninterrupted power for residential use.
